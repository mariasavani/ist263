Our Resturant Recomendations

Café Eiles


When in Austria stop by Café Eiles Cafe, Austrian for some Apfelstrudel!

Chez Léon


IF you are in Belgium be sure to grab some Moules Frites at Chez Leon. Brussels, Belgium.

Fritz European Fry House


Get yourself some delicious Poutine at Fritz European Fry House in Vancouver, British Columbia.

Heft


Want the full England experience? Stop by Heft, Lake District for some Steak and kidney pie!

Restaurant Saaga


If you want a true taste of Finland stop by Restaurant Saaga for some “squeeky cheese“ AKA Leipäjuusto.

Beefy's Aussie World


Hello Mate! You must grab s mest pie at Beefy’s when in Australia.

Gettó Gulyás


Get yourself some Goulash at Gettó Gulyás, in Budapest. Hungary, you won’t be sorry.

Migliori Olive Ascolane


When in Italy… don’t miss out on Migliori Olive Ascolane’s deep fried olives. A true delegacy!

Sobahouse Konjikihototogisu


Konjiki Hototogisu’s Ramen in Tokyo is something your taste buds won’t want to miss out on.

Pasja Smaku


You are going to want to get your hands on some Chlodnik at Pasja Smaku, in Poland.

Die Biltong Padstal


Die Biltong Padstal in South Africa has the best biltong around!
